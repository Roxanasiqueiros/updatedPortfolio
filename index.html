About

Roxana is a Content Analyst on the Search Ads Team at Google and a student at UC Berkeley Extension pursuing full stack programming.

In her current role as a content analyst, Roxana works with the search ads team to help them provide online users with quality marketing ads. Roxana reviews the ads and reports any errors or issues that violate the guidelines, and then she sends them of for a final QA check. This results in users having a positive experience when coming across the ads.

Roxana’s passion for learning and technology is what led her to pursue programming at UC Berkeley. She enjoys the challenges of learning new languages and concepts, learning full stack programming gives Roxana the freedom to be able to create new things and watch things come together from the front end to the back end.

Roxana’s discovered her passion for technology through Year Up, a one year intensive learning program. She completed 6 months of technical training in IT, and after completed a 6 month internship at Facebook as a marketing intern on the GMS App Ops Team. Roxana’s role at FB consisted of working with the internal teams to launch any information that they needed to share, either with their clients or with FB Users. She used Responsys an online marketing tool to distribute the information through email campaigns, and provided the teams a detailed report on the success of their campaigns.

Upon completion of the UC Berkeley program, Roxana will be searching for full stack developer roles to apply her programming skills as well as to advance them.
